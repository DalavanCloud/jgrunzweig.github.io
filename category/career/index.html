New Opportunities

  • by Josh Grunzweig
  • on Sun 08 March 2015

Just a quick post for today.

It's with quite a bit of excitement that I'm announcing a change of employment. Starting today, I'll be joining the Unit 42 group within Palo Alto Networks (PAN) as a Threat Intelligence Analyst. Threat intelligence is a slight departure from my historical focus, which was more geared towards assisting on forensics investigations, but it's certainly an area that I think will be both interesting and rewarding.

The Unit 42 group is fairly new (~1 year), and I'm really looking forward to working with such an amazing group of people. The passion towards innovation and security in general is very apparent the moment you begin speaking to Unit 42 and PAN in general. I suspect we'll have some fun in the coming months and there will be some valuable and interesting research being performed.
